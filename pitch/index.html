Mirio is a platform for creating interactive, cohort-based programming classes.

Vision

To make it easier for everyone* to teach and learn programming, from the classroom to the workplace.

* We’re starting with online programming classes for adults but our long-term vision extends to all technology-related education, from classroom learnining to corporate upskilling.

Problems for Teachers

There are many unnecessary barriers that make it hard to make money teaching.

  1. Most platforms require you to make videos. In addition to being a good teacher, you need the time, equipment, and skills to record and edit video.
  2. Existing platforms make it hard to make money. Udemy takes up to 75% and YouTube is even worse.
  3. If you want to create cohorts or interactive lessons, you need to build your own tools to host your curriculum, run live classes, manage your community, and take payments.

“I built my own platform from scratch. Most course creators don't have that... The hardest part by far has been the videos."

Popular course creator

Problems for Learners

Learning by watching videos is ineffective but bootcamps are expensive and inflexible.

  1. Learning to code over video is frustrating. Videos aren't interactive, requiring lots of tab switching and setup time (plus your own computer).
  2. It’s easy to get stuck and quit without the structure and peer / instructor support of a cohort.
  3. Bootcamps are very expensive with large, inflexible time commitments.

The biggest difference-maker [about the bootcamp] was the cohort. It was a lot harder for me to learn without mentorship and guidance."

Adult learner

Solution

We make it 10x easier to create and sell interactive, cohort-based classes.

Teachers

All of the buildling blocks you need to create and sell classes.

  1. Record lessons 10x faster in our recordable code editor (no video editing required).
  2. Build classes in our Notion-like curriculum builder.
  3. Run cohorts with chat and live classes.
  4. Built-in payments and distribution.

Learners

A marketplace of interactive classes with cohort-based support.

  1. Interact with your instructor's code in our playable editor. Create your own branches and projects.
  2. Get live instructor support and a cohort of peers for less than the price of a bootcamp.
  3. Access a wide array of classes across skill levels and languages, made possible by our marketplace.
